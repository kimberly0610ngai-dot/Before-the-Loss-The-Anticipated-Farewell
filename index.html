在失去之前：預期的告別 Before the Loss: The Anticipated Farewell

每當祖母再次因為病情反覆進出醫院，我總在日常的細碎瞬間中，提前預演失去的畫面。 Whenever my grandmother was hospitalized again, I rehearsed the experience of loss in everyday mundane moments.

哀傷是無聲的綵排，在心裡一遍遍上演，讓我提早哭泣和道別，也提早學會珍惜現在的每一刻。 Grief is a silent rehearsal—played again and again in the heart, allowing me to weep and bid farewell early.

展覽理念Exhibition Concept

本系列作品《Memento Mori》以「預期性悲傷」為核心，通過五個場景，具象化我在面對祖母反覆進出醫院的歲月中，內心所經歷的五個非線性情緒階段。 The series 'Memento Mori' centers on 'anticipatory grief'—five scenes materializing the nonlinear emotional phases I experienced during my grandmother's illness.

這不僅是個人的哀悼，更是一場視覺冥想，探討我們如何緊握、如何放手，以及如何在兩者間的空隙中尋得意義。 It is a visual meditation on how we hold on, how we let go, and how we find meaning in the space between.

理論背景：預期性悲傷Theoretical Framework

否認 DenialDenial

一種防禦性暫避，讓人以可承受的速度接受現實。A psychological buffer that allows individuals to accept reality at a manageable pace.

憤怒 AngerAnger

對不公與無力的情緒反應，核心在於深刻的無力感。Reflecting a profound sense of unfairness and powerlessness.

討價還價 BargainingBargaining

試圖透過與更高力量的談判來延後不可避免的結果。Seeking to regain control and postpone the inevitable through negotiation.

抑鬱 DepressionDepression

不再是憤怒，而是對即將到來的分離感到深沉哀傷。Appropriate, preparatory grief for the impending final separation.

接受 AcceptanceAcceptance

不是快樂，而是與現實達成和解，平靜地面對。Coming to terms with reality—a period of quiet preparation and peace.

四道人生The Four Paths of Life

善別，始於善生。A good farewell begins with living well.

道愛Love

將情感化為清晰、真摯的言語或行動，明確地傳遞給所愛之人。Expressing affection through sincere words and actions.

道謝Gratitude

誠心感謝生命中的幫助與陪伴，讓善意成為彼此的養分。Heartfelt thanks for the support and presence in one's life.

道歉Forgiveness

放下驕傲，尋求和解；釋懷傷痛，修復生命中的裂痕。Reconciling through humility and releasing past hurts.

道別Farewell

不是消極的永訣，而是帶著愛，繼續各自的旅程。A meaningful goodbye to carry forward with love.

關於藝術家Artist Bio

王藝寰

Wong Ngai Wan (Kimberli)

王藝寰 (Kimberli) 是香港教育大學藝術系學生，專精於混合媒介創作。其創作聚焦香港本土文化，致力喚起社會對城市重要議題的關注。 KIMBERLI Wong Ngai Wan is an art student at EdUHK specializing in mixed-media. Her work focuses on Hong Kong's local culture and social stories.

藝術家自述Artist Statement

「我的創作探索人類情感中抽象的失落過程。裝置《準備失去》並非誇張的哀悼，而是心靈過渡期中靜默而執著的印記。」 "My work explores the abstract processes of loss. These are not grand gestures of mourning, but the quiet, stubborn evidence of a heart in transition."
